Our Vision

Our core

African university of Benin is an incubation center for the evaluation and refining of conceived and perceived ideas needed for the methodical development of human society from different fronts.

Driven by Impact

The entrepreneurial orientation of our administrators coupled with their global focus has made the university the first choice for those who want to make an impact.

Practical Learning

We have created a paradigm shift to a more pragmatic, practical, and problem-based approach to teaching and learning for effective value creation.

Robust Expansion

We aim to become the first Pan-African university to operate in all the nations of Africa with a uniform standard that would bring the World to Africa.
